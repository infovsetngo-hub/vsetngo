About VSET NGO

VSET NGO is committed to transforming the lives of underprivileged communities. We provide education, skill development, career guidance, food, shelter, and essential support. Our vision is to build empowered communities where every individual can live with dignity and opportunity.

Our Services

Education Support

School tuition, scholarships, learning materials, digital classes, and mentorship programs for children and youth.

Skill Development

Vocational training, workshops, and hands-on skill development programs to increase employability and independence.

Career Guidance

Mentorship, career counseling, resume building, interview preparation, and internships to guide youth towards stable careers.

Food & Shelter

Providing nutritious meals, groceries, and safe temporary shelter for families and individuals facing hardship.

Basic Necessities

Distribution of clothing, hygiene kits, medical assistance, and other essential items to vulnerable communities.
